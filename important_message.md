In simple terms, here is exactly what the client wants:

1. The Quran in Every Single Language
Their ultimate goal is to have the Quran available in every language on earth. For rare or specific languages that are not available in standard Quran APIs, the client will manually find PDF files online and send them to you.

2. PDF Conversion & Formatting
The client wants you to take those PDF files and:

Convert/extract the text from the PDF.

Format it nicely so it matches the exact layout and look of the other languages on the platform.

Ensure proper Verse Mapping (making sure each translated sentence matches the correct Arabic Ayah and Surah).

3. Corresponding Tafsir (Exegesis)
They don't just want the translation; they also want the Tafsir to be available in that specific language. For example, if a user is reading in Hungarian, the Tafsir should also be translated into Hungarian or be available for that specific translation.
